    The Prophet’s Pray

    In worship, prayer is one of the most important practices. Because prayer is so important, in the Qur'an there are scattered prayers from prophets and apostles. In fact, in the collection of hadith, there are many prayers of the Prophet Muhammad saw. As the people of the Prophet Muhammad saw., it is appropriate to emulate the prayers of the Prophet Muhammad pbuh to be used and practiced in our daily lives. The Prophet’s Pray contains a collection of prayers that were successfully collected by Hamka. Prayers that are easy to memorize and recite are prayed for. The best offering of Hamka to Muslims.
