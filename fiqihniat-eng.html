    FIQH INTENTION

    The intention in every act of worship performed by a servant has a very important position. The fiqh scholars place intentions first before they describe the main points of fiqh that they will discuss. The intention possessed by a servant in carrying out a worship will lead to the results to be obtained. Everything a servant does will be worth worship depending on the intentions he has.

    This e-book is here to meet the needs of the people regarding an in-depth explanation of the fiqh of intentions. Consisting of six chapters, Umar Sulaiman al-Asyqar reviews matters related to intentions that are important in a worship, starting from its definition, taqlif, arguments, to the virtues and majesty of intentions. The discussion in this e-book is equipped with authentic hadiths to give the reader confidence in the explanation given.
