• Synopsis

    DIvorce: WEDDING EMERGENCY DOORS

    Marriage consists of two people with different backgrounds, experiences, and life principles. It's no wonder that in living domestic life it is very possible for misunderstandings, differences of opinion, and so on to occur.

    However, no matter how strong the shocks that occur in a marriage, they must try to be maintained when the shocks that occur are not things that deviate from religion. If it is really untenable, the divorce must be carried out according to the rules conveyed by Islam and properly considered.

    Reading this book will increase your knowledge about tips for dealing with household problems, especially the causes of divorce, Islamic rules regarding divorce, and also things you need to know about divorce. In addition, this book also explains about child custody when their parents divorce.
