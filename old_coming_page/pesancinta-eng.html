    LUQMAN AL-HAKIM'S LOVE MESSAGE: A WISE MAN IN THE QUR'AN

    He was Luqman bin `Anqa bin Sadun. Some say that his name is Luqman bin Tsaran. There are also those who say that his name is Ibn Ba`ur bin Nahir bin Aazir. Black man, thick lips, comes from the Ailah tribe in the Sudan, Egypt. A person who is blessed by Allah SWT but is not a prophet. There are also those who call him the wisest guardian. This e-book summarizes his wills written in the Qur'an Surah Luqmaan regarding prayer, admonishing ma`ruf nahi munkar, patience, and good character.
