• Synopsis

    Work and Business Etiquette

    In this modern era, the majority of us, both men and women, are required to have a job or a business (business). Various jobs and businesses are available in the field, and must be managed properly and optimally so that all types of work and business can continue to run within the syar`i corridor and provide beneficial benefits to all parties.

    This book is here so that readers can know and understand more about the adab (akhlaqiyat) that must be carried out, both by workers and business people. This book presents the various etiquette required by workers or business people—whether in working, managing a business, relationships with partners, or others—so that it is hoped that by understanding these adabs can eliminate the potential and risks of conflict that may arise due to a sense of injustice or conflict of interest between the parties involved in a business or business.
