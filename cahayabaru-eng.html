    The New Light

    The New Light book contains Hamka's thoughts and views on the struggle of the Indonesian nation in interpreting the independence of this country and all efforts to build it into a just and prosperous country. A land illuminated and shaded by a new light, Islamic values that became the grace of lil' alamiin. Independence that is upright above the blood and sacrifice of the whole nation and heroes, including Muslims and Islamic heroes of the motherland. Freedom that must continue to be interpreted and filled with noble values and noble ideals, namely freedom that exhorts man from servitude to his fellow creatures, except to Him.
