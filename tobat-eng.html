• Synopsis

    Repentance: Return to Allah

    Every soul always longs for love and always looks for love throughout his life. In this case, Ibn Qayyim al-Jauziyah wrote a way for us to get back the love of the Creator. He explains the intricacies of sin and repentance in vivid, detailed, and soul-touching ways.

    E-book Repentance: Return to Allah leads us to stay away from sin and immorality, understand the types of sin itself, and provide ways to leave immorality. Many parables are given by Ibn Qayyim so his narrative can be easier to understand and more permeated by the heart. This e-book invites us to return to the essence of the heart, which is to get true love from the Divine.
