• Synopsis

    Dhikr: The Light of Life

    Ibnu Qayyim al-Jauziyah gave us another provision for our worships with this exceptional creation. E-book Dhikr: The Light of Life told us about the new perspective of dhikr which is the easiest worship for Muslims but it contains an incredible value in the sight of Allah SWT. We often ignored dhikr and presume that is just a compliment of our worships but in this e-book dhikr becomes a priority. Ibnu Qayyim want to introduce the benefits of dhikr through this e-book.
