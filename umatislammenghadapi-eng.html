    Muslims Faced The Christianity and Secularism

    This book is a collection of Hamka's writings and sermons around the 60s and early 70s. Hamka highlighted the unrest of Muslims and Islamic religious leaders (clerics) against the christianization and secularization efforts that were rife at the time. By attacking the issue of aqidah and worship, they (the missionaries) want to shake the faith and faith of Muslims who are weak in religious knowledge. In fact, they persuaded Muslims who had economic difficulties to switch faiths. On the other hand, clash of civilizations and wars of thought (ghazwul fikri) are also vigorously launched by secularists against Muslims, especially in the younger generation of Muslims.
